**Role:** Programmer — Salty Studios (University of Toronto & Centennial College)
**Timeline:** September 2025 – present
**Website:** [bootleben.itch.io/salt-grooves-and-garlic](https://bootleben.itch.io/salt-grooves-and-garlic)

*Salt, Grooves, and Garlic* is an action-rhythm game in which players combat monsters to the beat of music inside procedurally generated dreamscapes. The project was developed by a 10+ person team and awarded **2nd place, Best Overall Game at Level Up Showcase 2026 (Presented by Zynga)**, a judging category that weighs mechanics, level design, interface, controls, user experience, and emotional impact.

My contributions as a programmer:

* Designed and implemented the rhythm-based input system, boss attack patterns, and the dynamic "Groove Meter" health/performance mechanic.
* Integrated 3D models, animations, and UI from the art team into Unity to deliver a cohesive gameplay experience.
* Participated in agile development cycles, including weekly stand-ups and playtesting sessions, iterating on the design based on user feedback.

**Tech:** Unity, C#.
