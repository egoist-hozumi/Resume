**Program:** Introduction to Computer Graphics Online Research Project
**Institution:** USC Viterbi School of Engineering
**Advisor:** Prof. Saty (DreamWorks Animation)
**Timeline:** February 2025 – April 2025

An immersive online research program focused on industry-standard computer graphics tooling and the integration of AI-driven content-creation techniques into the classical graphics pipeline.

Highlights:

* Worked fluently with **Blender, Maya, RenderMan, and THREE.js** to create, rig, and render 3D assets.
* Explored how image, video, and audio generation models can complement traditional rendering, shading, and animation pipelines.
* Led a team to deliver the final project, producing both a structured oral presentation and a comprehensive written report.
* Wrote substantial sections of the technical documentation, detailing 3D modeling processes and rendering pipelines.

Research output: *Towards Efficient LLMs: Analyzing Computational Bottlenecks and Optimization Strategies*, accepted by **AIDML 2025** (2025 3rd International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Database and Machine Learning).
